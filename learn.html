Your Carbon wallet can helps us to keep the environment save 🌍🔥


📌Carbon Footprint is like your "environment shadow" - how much harmful gases (CO2) your daily activities produce.


Activities That Add CO2:

  • 🚗 Too many old cars on road
  • 🔌 Overuse of generators
  • 🔥 Burning of waste & bush
  • 🌴 Cutting down trees (deforestation)

Call to action:

🚶 Transport

• Use BRT buses
• Trek short distances
• Driving less with private cars

💡 Energy

• Turn off unused lights
• Use solar lamps
• Fix leaking fridge

🍲 Food

• Eat more local foods
• Reduce meat 1 day/week
• Don't waste food

📚 Did You Know?

Nigeria loses 400,000 hectares of forest yearly - that's 500 football fields daily! 🌳➡️🏜️
